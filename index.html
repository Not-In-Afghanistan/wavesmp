Welcome to Wave SMP

A team? That word is a fairy tale told to keep the weak breathing. There is no “we.”
There is only you—thrown into a pit where everything moves with hunger and everything wants you gone.
You will be stalked. You will be stripped. Your work will be erased, your progress mocked, your name spat out like it never mattered.
This world does not teach. It punishes.
It does not forgive. It remembers.
Every moment is a test designed to break you, to see if you fold, to watch you disappear like the countless failures before you.
No laws shield you. No gods answer. No mercy exists.
So stand up anyway.
Burn hesitation out of your veins. Bury fear. Suffocate doubt.
Create without permission. Take without apology. Overwrite those who stood in your way.
Turn loss into fuel. Turn pain into power.
Let them watch as you outlast, outgrow, and overtake everything they thought would stop you.
Survival is not enough.
Ascend. Enforce your will. Leave nothing untouched.
Conquest is the only language this world understands.
